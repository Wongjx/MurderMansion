Privacy Policy

Murder Mansion

This policy explains what information Murder Mansion uses, why it is used, and how long it is kept. The current Android build requests internet access for online multiplayer and does not include active advertising or analytics SDKs.

  • Last updated: March 26, 2026
  • Applies to: current Murder Mansion app build and multiplayer service
  • Contact: GitHub project support

What We Collect

  • Display names that players enter for multiplayer rooms.
  • Room and session data such as room IDs, room codes, occupant IDs, ready status, and host state.
  • Network information needed to run online multiplayer, including IP address data processed by the discovery service.
  • Relay traffic sent during live multiplayer sessions so connected players can exchange game state.

What We Do Not Collect

  • No account registration is required to play the current build.
  • No active in-app ads are included in the current Android build.
  • No active third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs are included in the current Android build.
  • No camera, microphone, contacts, photo library, or location permissions are requested by the current Android build.

How Information Is Used

  • To create, join, and manage multiplayer rooms.
  • To route websocket relay connections between players during matches.
  • To show lobby information such as host name, player count, and room visibility.
  • To reduce abuse, including temporary kick lockouts for removed players.
  • To maintain service stability and basic network operations.

Retention

Multiplayer room records are intended to be temporary. The current discovery service closes inactive rooms after roughly 45 seconds without heartbeat activity.

Temporary kick-lockout records may be kept for up to 30 minutes to prevent immediate re-entry by removed players.

Sharing

Information is shared only as needed to operate multiplayer sessions. Other players may see your display name in room lobbies or public room listings if you host or join a public room.

The multiplayer service is currently hosted through Cloudflare infrastructure. Platform providers such as Apple and Google may also collect their own store or device data under their own privacy policies.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to limit data use to multiplayer operation, but no service can guarantee absolute security. Avoid using real names or other sensitive personal information as your in-game display name.

Your Choices

You can avoid sharing multiplayer data by not using online multiplayer features. If you do not want your display name shown to other players, do not enter personal information in that field.

Contact

Questions or requests about this policy can be sent through the project's support page: github.com/Wongjx/MurderMansion/issues

If you later add ads, analytics, account systems, or other third-party SDKs, update this policy before shipping that version.
